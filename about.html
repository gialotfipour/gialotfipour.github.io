Oh, hello there!

Hi, I’m Gia, a UX design student with a passion for research, accessibility, and crafting intuitive experiences. I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Interaction Design at Capilano University, specializing in Interaction Design, to bring together a story and design solutions that truly meet user needs. I believe in empathy-driven, data-backed design—understanding real people, their frustrations, and their goals, to create products that feel intuative and meaningful. My goal is to work with a company that values UX as a core driver of innovation and gives me the creative freedom to solve problems in new and impactful ways. Outside of design, I’m crazy for travel. Exploring new places allows me to connect with people I might’ve never met otherwise, and learn from their stories—an experience that deeply affects me as a person, and what I design for.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
